Companies

Track the organizations you do business with.

The Companies page lets you manage organizations in your pipeline. Navigate to it via Companies in the sidebar, or by visiting /demo/crm/companies.

Page Layout

  • Page title "Companies" with a total count.
  • A search bar filtering by company name and industry.
  • An "Add Company" button.

Companies are displayed as a responsive card grid (3 columns on desktop, fewer on smaller screens), not a table.

Company Fields

Field Required Description
Company NameYesName of the organization.
IndustryNoIndustry sector (e.g., "Technology", "Finance").
WebsiteNoCompany URL.
SizeNoOne of: startup, small, medium, or enterprise.
RevenueNoRevenue range as free text (e.g., "$1M - $5M").
AddressNoPhysical address.
StatusYesOne of: prospect, customer, or churned.

Company Statuses

  • prospect — An organization you are targeting but have not yet closed a deal with.
  • customer — An active paying customer.
  • churned — A former customer who is no longer active.

Company Cards

Each card in the grid shows:

  • A building icon and the company's status badge.
  • The company name in bold.
  • The industry in gray text below the name.
  • The number of associated contacts (e.g., "3 contacts").
  • The company size, if set.
  • Edit and Delete buttons at the bottom of the card.

Search

The search bar filters companies in real-time. It searches across company name and industry. The search is case-insensitive.

Adding a Company

  1. Click the "Add Company" button.
  2. A modal form appears. Fill in at least the Company Name.
  3. Optionally add Industry, Website, Size, Revenue, Address, and Status.
  4. Click "Add Company" to save.

Editing a Company

  1. Find the company card in the grid.
  2. Click the Edit button on the card.
  3. The modal opens pre-filled with the company's data.
  4. Make changes and click "Update Company".

Deleting a Company

  1. Find the company card in the grid.
  2. Click the Delete button on the card.
  3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog.
Note: Deleting a company does not delete contacts or deals associated with it. Those records will remain but will no longer reference the deleted company.
