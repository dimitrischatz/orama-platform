Contacts

Manage the people in your sales pipeline.

The Contacts page lets you store and manage information about individual people — leads, active customers, and past contacts. Navigate to it via Contacts in the sidebar, or by visiting /demo/crm/contacts.

Page Layout

At the top of the page you will see:

  • The page title "Contacts" with a count of total contacts.
  • A search bar that filters contacts in real-time.
  • An "Add Contact" button that opens the contact creation form.

Below is a table listing all contacts.

Contact Fields

Field Required Description
First NameYesContact's first name.
Last NameYesContact's last name.
EmailYesMust be a valid email address.
PhoneNoPhone number, any format.
CompanyNoDropdown of existing companies. Links this contact to a company.
TitleNoJob title (e.g., "VP Engineering", "CTO").
StatusYesOne of: lead, active, or inactive.

Contact Statuses

  • lead — A new prospect who has not yet become a customer.
  • active — A current, engaged contact or customer.
  • inactive — A contact that is no longer active or engaged.

Contacts Table

The table displays the following columns:

Column Details
NameFull name (first + last). The contact's job title appears as a subtitle beneath the name.
EmailEmail address.
CompanyThe associated company name, if any.
StatusColored badge showing lead, active, or inactive.
ActionsEdit (pencil icon) and Delete (trash icon) buttons.

Search

The search bar filters the contact list in real-time as you type. It searches across first name, last name, email, and company name simultaneously. The search is case-insensitive.

Adding a Contact

  1. Click the "Add Contact" button in the top-right corner.
  2. A modal form appears. Fill in at least the required fields: First Name, Last Name, and Email.
  3. Optionally select a Company from the dropdown, add a Phone number, Job Title, and set the Status.
  4. Click "Add Contact" at the bottom of the modal to save.

Editing a Contact

  1. Find the contact in the table.
  2. Click the pencil icon in the Actions column.
  3. The same modal opens, pre-filled with the contact's current data.
  4. Make your changes and click "Update Contact".

Deleting a Contact

  1. Find the contact in the table.
  2. Click the trash icon in the Actions column.
  3. A confirmation dialog appears. Confirm to permanently delete the contact.
Note: Deleting a contact does not automatically delete deals or activities linked to that contact. Those records will remain but will show the contact as unlinked.
