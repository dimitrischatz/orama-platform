AccelCRM Documentation

Your sales pipeline and customer relationship management platform.

AccelCRM helps sales teams manage their contacts, companies, deals, and day-to-day activities from a single interface. This documentation covers every feature of the platform and how to use it effectively.

Platform Overview

AccelCRM is organized into five main sections, accessible from the sidebar navigation:

Section Purpose
Dashboard At-a-glance metrics: total contacts, companies, pipeline value, and closed-won revenue. Also shows recent open deals and pending activities.
Contacts Manage individual people. Each contact has a name, email, phone, job title, associated company, and a status (lead, active, or inactive).
Companies Manage organizations. Each company has an industry, website, size classification, revenue range, and a status (prospect, customer, or churned).
Deals Track sales opportunities through a six-stage pipeline. Deals have a dollar value, win probability, and are linked to a contact and optionally a company. Viewable as a Kanban board or a list.
Activities Log and track calls, emails, meetings, tasks, and notes. Activities can be linked to a contact or deal and marked as completed.

Navigation

The left sidebar provides links to all sections. On mobile screens (under 1024px), the sidebar collapses behind a hamburger menu button in the top-left corner. Tap the button to open it, or tap outside to close it.

At the bottom of the sidebar you will find:

  • Back to Demos — returns to the demo selection screen.
  • Reset Demo — restores all data to its original seed state. A confirmation dialog is shown before resetting.

Key Concepts

Statuses

Both contacts and companies have status fields that indicate where they are in the customer lifecycle:

  • Contacts: lead → active → inactive
  • Companies: prospect → customer → churned

Pipeline Value

The pipeline value displayed on the dashboard is a weighted total. It is calculated by summing each open deal's value multiplied by its win probability. For example, a $100,000 deal at 50% probability contributes $50,000 to the pipeline value.

Data Relationships

Entities in AccelCRM are connected:

  • A Contact can belong to a Company.
  • A Deal is always linked to a Contact and optionally to a Company.
  • An Activity can be linked to a Contact, a Deal, or both.
