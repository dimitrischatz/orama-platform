Common Workflows

Step-by-step guides for typical CRM tasks.

Onboard a New Prospect

When a new potential customer reaches out, follow these steps to get them into the system:

  1. Go to Companies and click "Add Company". Enter the company name, industry, and set the status to prospect.
  2. Go to Contacts and click "Add Contact". Enter the person's details and select the company you just created from the Company dropdown. Set the status to lead.
  3. Go to Deals and click "Add Deal". Give it a title, estimated value, select the contact, and set the stage to Lead.
  4. Go to Activities and click "Add Activity". Create a call or meeting activity linked to the contact and deal, with a due date for your first outreach.

Advance a Deal Through the Pipeline

As you progress with a prospect, move the deal through stages:

  1. Navigate to the Deals page (Board view).
  2. Find the deal card in its current stage column.
  3. Drag the card to the next stage column (e.g., from "Lead" to "Qualified").
  4. The probability updates automatically. If needed, click Edit on the deal to adjust the probability or add notes.
  5. Log a follow-up activity on the Activities page to record what happened (e.g., "Discovery call completed") and plan next steps.

Close a Deal

When a deal reaches its conclusion:

  1. On the Deals board, drag the deal to Closed Won or Closed Lost.
  2. If won: go to Companies, find the company, click Edit, and change the status from prospect to customer.
  3. If won: go to Contacts, find the contact, click Edit, and change the status from lead to active.
  4. Log a note activity summarizing the outcome.

Track Daily Activities

Use the Activities page as your daily task list:

  1. Go to Activities and filter by Pending.
  2. Review your pending calls, emails, meetings, and tasks.
  3. As you complete each one, click the checkbox to mark it done.
  4. Add new activities for any follow-ups that arise.

Review Pipeline Health

Use the Dashboard to assess your overall pipeline:

  1. Go to the Dashboard.
  2. Check the Pipeline Value card — this is the weighted total of all open deals. If it is declining, you may need to add new deals or advance existing ones.
  3. Check the Closed Won card to see your total revenue from completed deals.
  4. Review the Open Deals section to see which deals are active and at what stage.
  5. Review the Pending Activities section to make sure nothing is overdue.

Update a Contact's Company

If a contact moves to a different organization:

  1. Make sure the new company exists. If not, go to Companies and add it first.
  2. Go to Contacts, find the contact, and click the Edit button.
  3. Change the Company dropdown to the new company.
  4. Update the contact's Title if their role has changed.
  5. Click "Update Contact" to save.

Reset the Demo

If you want to start fresh with the original sample data:

  1. Click "Reset Demo" at the bottom of the sidebar.
  2. A confirmation dialog will appear. Click Confirm.
  3. All contacts, companies, deals, and activities revert to the initial seed data.
Warning: Resetting the demo permanently discards all changes you have made. This action cannot be undone.
